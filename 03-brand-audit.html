Section 03

GSG Today — Brand Audit

How GSG presents itself in public — and the distance between what it says and what it has actually built.

How GSG speaks today

GIIS positioning statement

"The power to thrive, succeed & win in a globally connected but highly competitive world of opportunity."

Achievement-oriented. "Thrive" overlaps with Cognita's load-bearing word — GSG25 should move beyond it.

Live homepage hero

"Learn Limitless"

Sub: "A world-class learning experience designed for the future." The closing line of GSG's published manifesto — its articulated identity, not a campaign line.

GSG brand level

"GSG is a premier international K-12 education network… recognised as the World's Most Awarded Network of Schools."

Award-led, network-focused. "Premier" gestures at premium without fleshing it out.

Social media — brand fragmentation finding

GSG group Facebook: 838 followers. Individual GIIS campus pages: 6,000–15,000 each. @Global_Schools on X: 1,128 followers, zero posts. No campus social account carries the group headline or manifesto language. The brand identity exists on the website — but is far less obvious everywhere else. GSG25 is the activation opportunity to close this gap across all 64 campuses.

The published brand manifesto

The Limitless Experience

The world we live in is shrinking in size, expanding in possibilities and evolving at a blurring pace.

By forging connections, celebrating diversity, and opening minds. Breaking every geographic barrier and designing limitless classrooms. Creating endless possibilities for our global communities,

So that our students can become, not just professionals, but harbingers of a positive change. And architects of a better world.

This world is now calling you, to discover the endless potential in you. So you can transcend boundaries, expand your horizon and…

Learn Limitless

Published at globalschools.com/about/manifesto/ — and, the audit finds, deployed nowhere else. The 25th-anniversary campaign doesn't need a new idea; it needs to arm this one with 25 years of proof.

What GSG says vs. what GSG has built

The distance between the two is the opportunity. The most compelling story has not yet been told to the extent that it can be.

Says publicly

"Learn Limitless"

Has built

Non-selective schools producing results that outpace selective ones — a 37.2 IB average; 25 years of innovation as proof.

Says publicly

"Personalised learning"

Has built

SmartLearn — a proprietary, people-independent tech ecosystem protecting each child's learning continuity (45,000 students deployed).

Says publicly

"Global network"

Has built

A genuinely connected network — shared pedagogy, cross-campus exchange, joint innovation summits.

Says publicly

"International curriculum with local roots"

Has built

GIIS Tokyo: opened 2006 with 60 students — today over 1,300, the majority Japanese local families. (Japan Times, Dec 2023.)

Says publicly

"Innovation"

Has built

GJET — a K-12 student research publication platform with no comparable programme identified globally.
