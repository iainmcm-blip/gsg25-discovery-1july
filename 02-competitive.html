Flag · terminology conflict

"Thrive" is already partially occupied by Cognita

Cognita's purpose statement

"Thrive in a rapidly evolving world"

Confirmed on cognita.com, Jacobs Capital & Sofina portfolio pages, Reuters (Feb 2024) and multiple TES listings.

GIIS current positioning

"The power to thrive, succeed & win…"

"Thrive" appears as a primary verb in the positioning currently distributed to all schools (GIIS brand guidelines).

Recommendation: be careful using "thrive" across any GSG25 messaging. It is Cognita's focus brand word. GSG must use language that is unambiguously its own.

The competitive map

What is claimed, what is crowded, and what is genuinely open to GSG.

Open Partially open Occupied Crowded
Open

Accessible excellence — quality without premium pricing

GSG's most distinctive, verifiable territory. No benchmarked global group combines accessible fees with GSG's level of non-selective academic proof.

Occupied

"Learn Limitless" / limitless learning

Occupied on three fronts: GSG's own manifesto, its homepage hero, and GESS Singapore's "Limitless Learning" 2023–2029 plan. A decision is needed.

Open

Non-selective with outstanding academic results

Inspired notes non-selective as proof. GSG's frame — any child transformed into an exceptional learner — is harder, more impressive and unowned.

Open

Singapore-born, globally connected, locally rooted

No competitor has a Singapore founding story. The "local international school" identity has no competitive equivalent.

Partially open

Proprietary integrated tech ecosystem (SmartLearn)

ISP has a methodology; Cognita has Flint. Neither claims a 23-year proprietary, people-independent tech stack at GSG's scale.

Open

"World's most awarded network" — 600+ awards

No competitor prominently claims award-volume leadership. Available, uncontested and independently verifiable.

Occupied

"Thrive" as a primary brand verb

Cognita has claimed this word — confirmed across cognita.com, jacobscap.com, sofina.com and Reuters. Avoid in GSG25 headline language.

Crowded

Academic excellence / world-class / global citizens

Used by all five in various forms. Table-stakes language — insufficient to differentiate on its own.

Sources: nordangliaeducation.com · gemseducation.com · internationalschoolspartnership.com · cognita.com · inspirededu.com · globalschools.com · limitless.gess.edu.sg.
