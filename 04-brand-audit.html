Section 04

GSG Today — Brand Audit

The distance between what GSG has built and how it presents itself is the opportunity. The most compelling story has not yet been told to the extent it can be — and GSG25 is the moment to close the gap.

What GSG says vs. what GSG has built

GSG's own homepage uses "Learn Limitless" as its hero — without yet the 25-year evidence base to give it substance.

Says

"Learn Limitless"

Has built

Non-selective schools producing results that outpace selective ones — a 37.2 IB average; 25 years of innovation as proof. The headline can be given specific, owned meaning no competitor can match.

Says

"Personalised learning"

Has built

SmartLearn — a proprietary, people-independent tech ecosystem protecting each child's learning continuity (45,000 students deployed).

Says

"Global network"

Has built

A genuinely connected network — shared pedagogy, cross-campus exchange, joint innovation summits.

Says

"International curriculum with local roots"

Has built

GIIS Tokyo: opened 2006 with 60 students — today over 1,300, the majority Japanese local families. (Japan Times, Dec 2023.)

Says

"Innovation"

Has built

GJET — a K-12 student research publication platform with no comparable programme identified globally.

The opportunity

Arm the homepage line with 25 years of proof, so it means something no competitor can copy.

Make SmartLearn the visible signature of "personalised at scale" — a true category of one.

Show the network as one living system, not a logo set — the connective tissue is the story.

Lead with the rise of "local" students (Tokyo) as evidence that accessible excellence travels.

Own "research from age 5" as concrete proof of ambition without a price tag.
