What this deck establishes

Six findings — from the sector opportunity to the decision required today.

01 · The opportunity is now

14,833+ international schools globally, 58% in Asia. Fee income $67.3B — up 22% since 2020. The local learner is the growth story, and GSG sits at the centre of every trend that matters.

02 · Competitors avoid the hard claim

Nord Anglia and Inspired own premium performance. GEMS owns scale, access and legacy. Cognita and ISP lean holistic. None combine accessible fee positioning with GSG's level of non-selective academic proof.

03 · GSG already occupies it

A 37.2 IBDP average (non-selective). 600+ awards. 25 years. A published manifesto, "The Limitless Experience," and a homepage reading "Learn Limitless" — clearly articulated, but not consistently deployed.

04 · Research has confirmed the territory

Three depth interviews and a staff survey independently confirm Accessible Excellence — outstanding outcomes available to any child, without premium pricing or selective entry.

05 · The decision required today

Confirm agreement with the recommended strategic territory, so the work can move to activation and the second Atul briefing.

06 · What happens immediately after

A meeting next week to present activation directions for confirmation, while Motion proceeds to flesh out the detail within the Masterbrand Guide.
